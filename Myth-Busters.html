Myth Busters

Straight answers to common questions — no hype, no filters, just facts.

“Botox freezes your face.”

In skilled, conservative dosing the goal is to soften lines while keeping natural expression. “Frozen” looks come from over-treating or poor technique — not an inevitable outcome. We specialise in subtle, natural results.

“Fillers last forever.”

Hyaluronic acid fillers are temporary and gradually metabolised. Typical duration ranges from ~6–18 months depending on product, area and individual factors.

“You can’t work or exercise after.”

Most people return to normal life immediately. We’ll give simple aftercare (avoid heavy exercise or facials for a short period) to support the best result.

“One size fits all.”

Everyone’s muscles, skin and goals are different. We dose and plan conservatively based on your assessment, then review and refine where appropriate.

Myth Busters

Common myths, answered

  • “I’ll look frozen.” Treatment is tailored — not all-or-nothing. The aim is softening movement, not removing it. Some people want a more subtle effect; some want stronger relaxation. Both are valid, and this is discussed at assessment.
  • “Everyone gets the same dose.” Dose planning is individual — based on your muscle strength, goals, and facial anatomy. Two people with the same “area” can need different unit amounts.
  • “Results last forever.” Effects are temporary. Most people get 3–4 months from treatment. This is why regular top-ups maintain the result — not because anything is “wearing out” or “damaging” your face.
  • “Botox removes all emotion.” The aim is to reduce harsh / fixed expression lines — not your ability to look happy, surprised, or expressive. Treatment should still look like you.
  • “Only women get anti-wrinkle treatment.” Not true. Around 10% of clients nationally are men — and this proportion is increasing every year.
  • “It works instantly.” Most people start to notice change around day 3–5, with full effect at ~14 days. This is why your review is scheduled after this settling period.
  • “Top-ups are automatically free.” Reviews are included. If more units are clinically needed, they may require a new single-patient-use vial. This is discussed transparently first.
  • “If you start, you can’t stop.” You can. Stopping treatment simply means your muscles gradually return to normal strength, and your lines return as they were — not worse than before.

If you have a myth you’d like answered — add it to your Pre-Appointment form and it will be discussed during your consultation.
