Myth Busters

Honest, evidence-based answers about Botox and fillers — from an Advanced Clinical Practitioner and Independent Prescriber.

The most common myths — explained clearly

If you’re unsure what’s true, you’re not alone. Here are straight answers to the questions I’m asked most.

Myth: Botox freezes your face

Fact: When dosed and placed correctly, anti-wrinkle treatment softens lines while keeping your natural expression. “Frozen” results usually come from over-treatment.

Myth: Fillers always look obvious

Fact: Subtle volumising can refresh without changing your features. The goal is balance and proportion — not a different face.

Myth: It’s unsafe unless a doctor does it

Fact: Safety comes from training, assessment and scope of practice. I’m an Advanced Clinical Practitioner and Independent Prescriber with long-standing NHS experience and specific training in injectables.

Myth: Downtime is guaranteed

Fact: Most people return to normal activity the same day. You’ll get personalised aftercare to minimise swelling and bruising.

Myth: It’s addictive

Fact: Results wear off slowly. You choose whether and when to repeat treatment. My approach is conservative and evidence-based.

Myth: One size fits all

Fact: Every face is different. Your plan is tailored in consultation — sometimes the best plan is to do nothing.
