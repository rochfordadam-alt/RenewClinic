Myth Busters

Straight answers to common questions — no hype, no filters, just facts.

“Botox freezes your face.”

In skilled, conservative dosing the goal is to soften lines while keeping natural expression. “Frozen” looks come from over-treating or poor technique — not an inevitable outcome. We specialise in subtle, natural results.

“Fillers last forever.”

Hyaluronic acid fillers are temporary and gradually metabolised. Typical duration ranges from ~6–18 months depending on product, area and individual factors.

“You can’t work or exercise after.”

Most people return to normal life immediately. We’ll give simple aftercare (avoid heavy exercise or facials for a short period) to support the best result.

“One size fits all.”

Everyone’s muscles, skin and goals are different. We dose and plan conservatively based on your assessment, then review and refine where appropriate.

“If you start, you can’t stop.”

You can stop any time. Muscles gradually return to normal activity, and lines return as they were over time — not worse because you had treatment.

“Top-ups are automatically free.”

Reviews are included to assess results. If additional units are clinically indicated, they may require opening a new single-patient-use vial — agreed transparently first.

Natural results

Does Botox® freeze your face?

No. In conservative dosing the aim is to soften lines while keeping natural expression. We specialise in subtle, “you but fresher” results — not a frozen look.

Duration

How long do anti-wrinkle results last?

Typically around 3–4 months on average, varying by dose, area treated and individual factors. Results fade gradually — it isn’t permanent.

Duration

How long do dermal fillers last?

Most hyaluronic acid fillers last roughly 6–18 months depending on the product, area and your own metabolism.

Dose & pricing

Will I need a second vial?

Sometimes, based on muscle strength and goals. If appropriate, we’ll agree it with you first. Any additional vials are priced separately — see Pricing.

Follow-up

Do you offer a review?

For anti-wrinkle treatments we offer an optional review around 2–3 weeks to check progress and make small adjustments if needed.

Aftercare

Is there downtime?

Most people go straight back to normal activities. You may have small bumps or redness that settle quickly, and occasional pinpoint bruising. We’ll give clear aftercare guidance.

Comfort

Does it hurt?

Treatments are typically very quick with minimal discomfort — often described as tiny pinpricks. We use gentle technique and appropriate comfort measures.

Medical considerations

Who is not suitable?

We don’t treat during pregnancy or breastfeeding, and some medical conditions/medications mean treatment may not be appropriate. We’ll screen this during consultation to prioritise your safety.
