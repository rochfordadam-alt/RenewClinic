Myth Busters

Straightforward answers to common questions.

“Anti-wrinkle injections will freeze my face.”

Not when dosed and placed carefully. The aim is softening movement, not removing expression. Your plan is tailored to your goals.

“Fillers always look obvious.”

Poor placement or over-filling looks obvious. Subtle volume with CE-marked HA fillers can look natural and hydrated, not “done”.

“I can’t go out afterwards.”

Most people have only mild redness or tiny marks that settle quickly. We’ll give you simple aftercare and what to expect.

“I’m worried about safety.”

Every treatment begins with a medical assessment, consent and photography. You’ll get clear aftercare, and we’re here if you have questions.
