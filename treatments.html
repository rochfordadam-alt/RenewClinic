Upper face

Anti-wrinkle treatment

Softens dynamic lines caused by muscle movement — while keeping your expression natural. Review at ~2 weeks included.

Good for

  • Frown lines (glabella)
  • Forehead lines (treated with glabella for brow support)
  • Crow’s feet (both eyes count as one area)

What to expect

  • Treatment time ~20–30 minutes
  • Onset 3–5 days, full effect ~14 days
  • Typical longevity ~3–4 months

Safety & dosing

Dosing is based on your assessment (muscle strength, anatomy, goals). A single 50-unit, single-patient vial is included per appointment; if more units are needed, opening a second vial is discussed first (£65 supply fee).

Jaw

Masseter (clenching / grinding)

Targets the masseter muscles to reduce jaw clenching and gradually soften bulky jaw contours.

Good for

  • Teeth grinding (bruxism) and jaw tension
  • Square or heavy jawline from muscle hypertrophy

What to expect

  • Treatment time ~20–30 minutes
  • Onset 1–2 weeks; review at 6–8 weeks
  • Longevity typically 3–5 months (varies)

Dose is individual; effects are muscular and don’t replace dental care where indicated.

Sweat control

Underarm sweating (hyperhidrosis)

Reduces sweat gland activity in the underarms for longer-lasting dryness and comfort.

Good for

  • Persistent underarm sweating affecting daily life
  • Event prep (weddings, presentations, performance)

What to expect

  • Treatment time ~30 minutes
  • Onset 1–2 weeks; duration can be several months

Suitability checked in consultation. Aftercare keeps the area clean and product undisturbed for the first day.

HA Fillers

Dermal fillers

Hyaluronic acid (HA) fillers can restore volume, refine contours and soften creases while keeping balance and proportion.

Common areas

  • Lip definition and volume
  • Nasolabial folds and marionette lines

What to expect

  • Treatment time ~30–45 minutes
  • Immediate result; settles over 1–2 weeks
  • Longevity depends on area/product (typically months)

CE-marked HA products used. Full medical assessment and consent required.
