Treatments

Every treatment begins with a clinician-led medical assessment and a shared decision on the best plan for you.

Anti-wrinkle (upper face)

Targets dynamic lines such as frown, forehead and crow’s feet with precise, individualised dosing.

  • Assessment of muscle activity and skin quality
  • Treatment plan tailored to your goals
  • Review appointment included

Teeth grinding (masseter)

Helps reduce jaw clenching and grinding, easing tension and softening the masseter bulk gradually.

  • Outcomes build over weeks; review included
  • May improve jaw comfort and reduce wear

Underarm sweating

Injections reduce sweat activity in the underarm area. Typical results last several months.

  • Assessment and mapping included
  • Personalised aftercare guidance

Dermal fillers

Subtle volume and refinement for lips and smile lines using CE-marked hyaluronic acid fillers.

  • Medical consultation & photography
  • Gradual, natural-looking results
