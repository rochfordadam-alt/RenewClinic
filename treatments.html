Upper face

Upper-face anti-wrinkle

Softens dynamic lines caused by muscle movement — while keeping your expression natural. Review at ~2 weeks included.

Good for

  • Frown lines (glabella)
  • Forehead lines (treated with glabella for brow support)
  • Crow’s feet (both eyes count as one area)

What to expect

  • Treatment time ~20–30 minutes
  • Onset 3–5 days, full effect ~14 days
  • Typical longevity ~3–4 months

Safety & dosing

Dosing is based on your assessment (muscle strength, anatomy, goals). A single 50-unit, single-patient vial is included per appointment; if more units are needed, opening a second vial is discussed first (£65 supply fee).

Lower face

Lower-face anti-wrinkle

Subtle refinements to soften and balance the lower face — always tailored to your anatomy and goals.

May be suitable for

  • Softening a downturned mouth corner appearance
  • Chin dimpling / “pebble chin” look
  • A gentle lip flip for a slightly more defined upper lip shape
  • “Bunny lines” across the nose when smiling

What to expect

  • Treatment time ~20–30 minutes
  • Results typically appear over 3–14 days (area dependent)
  • Longevity varies (often ~2–4 months)

These options are assessed in person and only offered if appropriate. If it’s not the right fit, I’ll tell you honestly and suggest alternatives.
