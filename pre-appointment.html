Pre-Appointment

How to prepare, what to expect, parking and aftercare — all in one place.

Before you come

Please complete the short Pre-Appointment form so we can review your medical history and goals. Avoid alcohol and strenuous exercise on the day where possible, and arrive with clean skin (no heavy makeup on treatment areas).

What to expect

We’ll start with a consultation and assessment. If treatment is suitable and you wish to proceed, we’ll explain everything and take consent. Most treatments are quick with minimal discomfort.

Parking & access

Street parking is available locally. If you need a visitor permit, we can activate one for your appointment — just add your registration when you book. Please note there are steps at the entrance and the room is not wheelchair accessible.

Aftercare

You’ll receive simple aftercare tailored to your treatment. Most people return to normal life immediately; minor redness or pinpoint bruising can occur and settles quickly.
