Safety & Aftercare

Clear risks, realistic expectations and simple aftercare — always.

Anti-wrinkle injections — risks & side effects

  • Common: mild redness, small bumps, tenderness, transient headache (settle within 24–48h)
  • Less common: bruising, swelling, asymmetry, eyelid/eyebrow heaviness (temporary)
  • Rare: infection, allergy. Seek advice if symptoms persist or worsen.

Dermal fillers — risks & side effects

  • Common: redness, swelling, tenderness, bruising
  • Less common: lumps/unevenness (often resolve with massage or review)
  • Important but rare: vascular compromise (blanching, severe pain, colour change). Contact us urgently if concerned.

Teeth grinding / masseter — notes

  • Jaw function may feel different while clenching reduces
  • Full effect develops gradually over weeks; review included

Underarm sweating — notes

  • Results typically last several months; varies by individual
  • Avoid tight clothing and vigorous exercise on the day

Aftercare — general

  • Keep treated areas clean and avoid touching for 4–6 hours
  • No strenuous exercise, saunas or facials on the day
  • Sleep on your back if possible the first night
  • Make-up: ideally avoid for 4–6 hours; use clean tools if applying
  • If bruising occurs, cold compresses can help
  • If you feel unwell or worried, contact us: info@renewclinic.uk
