Safety

HCPC-registered, evidence-based practice and clear, informed consent.

Credentials

Clinician-led care

All consultations and treatments are carried out by Adam Rochford — Advanced Musculoskeletal Clinician & Independent Prescriber (HCPC registered). Care is evidence-based, conservative and focused on your long-term safety.

Governance

Medical assessment first

Before any treatment, we take a full medical history and talk through your goals, expected outcomes, alternatives and potential risks. Treatment only goes ahead if it’s safe and appropriate for you.

Informed choice

Consent & aftercare

You’ll receive clear, honest information about what to expect, aftercare and any possible side effects. Written aftercare guidance and follow-up reviews are provided where appropriate.

Suitability

When we won’t treat

For safety reasons, we do not provide aesthetic treatments during pregnancy or while breastfeeding.

We may also advise against treatment, or recommend delaying it, if there are medical factors that increase risk. These can include (but are not limited to):

  • Active skin infection, inflammation or injury in the treatment area
  • Known allergies or sensitivities to treatment ingredients
  • Certain neurological, neuromuscular or autoimmune conditions
  • Uncontrolled medical conditions or feeling acutely unwell
  • Recent facial surgery or procedures where healing is still ongoing
  • Medicines that may affect safety or treatment outcomes

If treatment isn’t right for you, we’ll always explain why and discuss alternative options where possible. Your safety and wellbeing come first.

Prescription-only medicines are not advertised by brand. Individual results vary. We take a conservative, natural-first approach — enhancing your features without changing who you are.
