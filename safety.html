Safety

HCPC-registered, evidence-based practice and clear, informed consent.

Credentials

Clinician-led care

All consultations and treatments are carried out by Adam Rochford — Advanced Musculoskeletal Clinician & Independent Prescriber (HCPC registered). Extensive experience with safe injectable techniques, clinical governance and patient-centred care.

Governance

Medical assessment first

Before any treatment we take a full medical history and discuss your goals, expected outcomes, alternatives and risks. Treatment only proceeds if clinically appropriate and safe for you.

Informed choice

Consent & aftercare

You’ll receive clear information on benefits, risks, what to expect and aftercare. We provide written guidance and a follow-up review where appropriate.

Suitability

When we won’t treat

We do not treat during pregnancy or breastfeeding and may defer or decline treatment where medical factors or goals suggest a poor risk-benefit balance. Your safety comes first.

Prescription-only medicines are not advertised by brand. Individual outcomes vary. We take a conservative, natural-first approach — subtle improvements that still look like you.
