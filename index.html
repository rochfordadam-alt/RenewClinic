Meet Adam

Advanced Clinical Practitioner (MSK) • Independent Prescriber (HCPC registered)

I’m Adam Rochford — an Advanced Clinical Practitioner in musculoskeletal health, with over 20 years’ experience in the NHS. I hold a Physiotherapy degree, a Master’s degree, a Diploma in Injection Therapy, and I’m an Independent Prescriber.

Throughout my career, I’ve performed many hundreds of injectable procedures, and spent eight years as a Clinical and Operational Lead within the Orthopaedic Interface Service and lead multidisciplinary teams of more than 40 clinicians.

I now bring the same evidence-based, precise and conservative clinical approach to aesthetics — focusing on subtle, natural results that enhance your features while still looking like you.
