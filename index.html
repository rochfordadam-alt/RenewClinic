FAQs

Why do you say “anti-wrinkle injections” instead of brand names?

UK rules don’t allow clinics to advertise prescription-only medicines to the public. We use generic terms and provide personalised advice during a clinician-led consultation.

Do you offer treatment for underarm sweating?

Yes — we provide a clinician-led anti-sweating treatment following assessment and informed consent.

Who performs the treatment?

All consultations and treatments are carried out by a registered clinician with appropriate training and insurance.
