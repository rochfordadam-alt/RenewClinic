Where we treat

Clinic location

Appointments are offered at a private clinic room in the Entry Hill area of Bath. The full address and directions are provided in your booking confirmation.

We operate by appointment only to protect your privacy and to maintain a calm, unhurried experience.

Parking

Street parking is available locally. If you need a visitor permit, we can activate one for your appointment — just add your vehicle registration when you book. (Optional field on our pre-appointment form.)

Accessibility: Please note: the clinic room has steps at the entrance and is not wheelchair accessible.

Flexible care

Home visits (BaNES)

If it’s easier for you, we can provide mobile appointments across BaNES for suitable treatments. Availability is limited and depends on clinical suitability, environment, and safe handling of prescription medicines. We’ll discuss this at consultation.

Attend with a companion

Come with a friend

You’re welcome to attend with a friend or family member. Dual consultations and treatments can be arranged where appropriate so you feel comfortable and supported.

All treatments follow a clinician-led medical assessment to ensure suitability and safety. Prescription-only medicines are not advertised by brand name. Results vary with individual muscle strength, skin quality and aftercare. If a second vial is required for your goals or muscle strength, the £65 vial fee will be agreed with you in advance during consultation or review.
